Our Mission

MentorX is a non-profit organization dedicated to uplifting Asian startup founders and other underrepresented groups within the startup ecosystem.

Through our mentorship program, we provide invaluable guidance, support, and networking opportunities to cultivate an inclusive landscape where every entrepreneur can thrive, innovate, and shape the future of the startup world.

By fostering a community that values diversity and embraces collaboration, we strive to break down barriers, unlock potential, and build a more equitable and vibrant entrepreneurial ecosystem for all.

Our Programs

Our dedicated mentorship program connects founders with experienced mentors who provide guidance and support tailored to the unique challenges faced by underrepresented groups.

Join us bi-monthly for the Seattle Startup Showcase, where founders can pitch their startups, receive coaching from mentors, and connect with fellow founders. Learn more.

Founders can request office hours to have one-on-one sessions with our mentors, gaining personalized insights and advice. Join our Discord to schedule a session.
