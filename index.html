Our Mission

Uplifting Underrepresented Startup Founders

MentorX is a non-profit organization dedicated to uplifting Asian startup founders and other underrepresented groups within the startup ecosystem.

Mentorship and Community Support

Through our mentorship program, we provide invaluable guidance, support, and networking opportunities to cultivate an inclusive landscape where every entrepreneur can thrive, innovate, and shape the future of the startup world.

Fostering Diversity and Collaboration

We foster a community that values diversity and embraces collaboration by breaking down barriers, unlocking potential, and building a more equitable and vibrant entrepreneurial ecosystem for all.

Our Programs

Our dedicated mentorship program connects founders with experienced mentors who provide guidance and support tailored to the unique challenges faced by underrepresented groups.

  • Mentorship Program: Experienced mentors provide tailored guidance and support to help founders overcome unique challenges.
  • Seattle Startup Showcase: Held bi-monthly, this event allows founders to pitch their startups, receive coaching from mentors, and connect with fellow founders.
  • Office Hours: Founders can request one-on-one sessions with mentors to gain personalized insights and advice. Join our Discord to schedule a session.
